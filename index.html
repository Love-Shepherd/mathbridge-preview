Reading comes first

School introduces students to mathematics. Learning to read it takes a lifetime.

Students are asked to read mathematics long before anyone teaches them how. So we begin there, with the definitions, diagrams and proofs they are usually rushed past, and we slow down until each one makes sense.

It is the step most teaching skips. It is the one we start with.

The curriculum

Three courses. One path.

Every student begins with Read Like a Mathematician, and everything grows from there. Each course builds gently on the one before, and asks a little more.
