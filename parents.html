For parents

An education that lasts beyond the exam.

You want an education that changes how your child thinks, and gives them the confidence to learn on their own.

Every parent hopes their child will meet a teacher who changes how they see a subject by showing what makes it worth studying. That is the education we offer.

Many of our students already enjoy mathematics. They want more of it than the classroom can give.

How we teach

Every lesson begins with conversation.

Students explain their thinking, ask questions and come back to important ideas from new angles, until mathematics becomes something to talk about as much as something to solve.

Our aim is not only to prepare your child for university. It is to help them arrive with habits of learning that stay with them.

Begin with Read Like a Mathematician

Every mathematical education begins somewhere. At MathBridge, it begins with reading.

Read Like a Mathematician is the foundation of the curriculum and the starting point for every student.
