Admissions

Every student begins with Read Like a Mathematician.

It introduces the habits of reading, writing and thinking that everything else builds on. It is where every student starts.

The process
I

Submit an application

Tell us about yourself, your background in mathematics and what brings you to MathBridge.

II

Application review

We read every application ourselves. If we think MathBridge is right for you, we will invite you to meet us.

III

Introductory meeting

A friendly conversation between the student, the parent and Lerato. A chance to ask questions and decide together whether MathBridge is the right place.

IV

Offer of admission

If a place is offered, you will receive everything you need to begin.

Before you apply
