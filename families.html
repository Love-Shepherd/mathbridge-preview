For families

An education that outlasts school.

Our focus is not simply on examination performance. It is on helping students develop the habits of mind that support long term academic success and intellectual growth.

Students leave with a portfolio of their own thinking, a record of intellectual growth, and a deeper confidence in their ability to engage with challenging ideas.

More than tutoring

We do not cram for tests or patch last term’s gaps. We teach your child a way of thinking that serves them long after the syllabus is forgotten, at university and beyond.

A small, considered cohort

Each cohort is limited to eight students, admitted by application and composed thoughtfully by stage. Your child learns beside genuine peers, not in a crowd.

Care and composition

School-age and university students are taught in separate cohorts. Where a younger student is ready for more, they may join an older group only with your consent. Every session is held to clear standards of conduct, and every session is recorded.

Taught by the founder

Every cohort is taught personally by Lerato Molisana: a Fulbright Scholar, with a BSc in Mathematical Statistics and an MFA in Creative Writing.
