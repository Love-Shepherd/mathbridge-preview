For families

An education that outlasts the examination.

If you are here, you are looking for something more considered than tutoring. So are we.

We do not repair marks. We give your child a way of thinking that lasts: precision, judgement, the confidence to sit with a hard idea until it yields.

Enrichment, not remediation

Your child need not be struggling. Many of our students already excel and want more.

Taught one to one

Each student is mentored privately by the founder, and meets fortnightly with a small group of peers to read and discuss a great mathematical life.

For the age of AI

In a world being reshaped by machines, we teach the one thing that holds its value: the ability to think.

What your child carries forward

A portfolio of their own thinking: evidence no transcript captures and an advantage in any application.

Taught by the founder

Every student is taught personally by Lerato Molisana, a mathematician, a writer and a Fulbright Scholar.
