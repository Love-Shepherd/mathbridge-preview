About

Lerato Molisana

I am a mathematician, a writer, and a teacher.

The three have never felt separate to me.

I studied Mathematical Statistics before completing a Master of Fine Arts in Creative Writing as a Fulbright Scholar in the United States.

MathBridge grew from a simple observation: mathematics behaves like a language.

Why I teach

I have watched capable students decide, quietly, that mathematics was not for them. It almost always happened at a sentence no one had taught them how to read. I built MathBridge for them. The ones who were always good at this, until the page changed.
