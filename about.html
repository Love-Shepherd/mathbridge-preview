About

Lerato Molisana

I am a mathematician and a writer.

I hold a BSc in Mathematical Statistics from the University of the Free State and an MFA in Creative Writing from Northern Arizona University, completed with distinction as a Fulbright Scholar. Both disciplines taught me the same thing. In writing, we learn to read closely and write with care. In mathematics, we are taught only to calculate. Yet mathematics is a language too.

MathBridge began there. Before a student can think mathematically, they must learn to read mathematics.

Why I teach

I have taught capable students who sensed there was more to the subject than they had been shown. There was. I built MathBridge for them and for those whose curiosity runs past the syllabus.
