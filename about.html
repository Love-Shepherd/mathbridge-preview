About

Lerato Molisana

I became a mathematics teacher because I loved mathematics. I founded MathBridge because I grew fascinated by how students learn.

Teaching high school mathematics, alongside years of tutoring, brought me close to hundreds of students. Over time, I came to see that success in mathematics depends on far more than mastering procedures. Students flourish when they learn to read closely, think independently and communicate their reasoning with confidence.

My academic life has been shaped by two disciplines. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Mathematical Statistics and a Master of Fine Arts in Creative Writing, completed with distinction as a Fulbright Scholar. Although these fields are often treated as separate, I have always experienced them as closely connected. Both ask me to interpret closely, express ideas clearly and pursue understanding with discipline.

MathBridge grew from that intersection, and now brings the two together through mathematics.

The role of a teacher is not only to explain difficult ideas. It is to help students discover they can understand them for themselves.
