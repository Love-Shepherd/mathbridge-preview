The Rooted Mathematician.

The Rooted Mathematician is my journal and newsletter. I write here about mathematics as a language, and about the books, ideas and questions that shape how I think and teach. It is for readers who believe mathematics is meant to be understood, and returned to, slowly.

The Oldest LanguageMathematics is older than writing itself. This essay explains why it is a language, and what we gain when we learn to read it, rather than only solve it.
Why Good Students Never Meet MathematicsAn exploration of mathematics and creative writing, asking what we lose when we teach formulas before meaning.
