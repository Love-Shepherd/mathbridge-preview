The learning experience

Every lesson is taught personally by Lerato.

Lessons are built around conversation, close reading and working through problems together. Students keep their own notes, return to the ideas that matter, and grow confident enough to face unfamiliar mathematics on their own.

The full path

Read, then write, then think.

A MathBridge education is meant to be taken in full. Each course prepares the ground for the next, and the three together are a single progression. The student who completes all three leaves not only stronger at mathematics, but a young scholar, ready for university and for work of their own.
