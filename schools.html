For schools

Mathematics beyond the classroom.

We work with schools that want to give their students more than the syllabus alone can offer.

Our workshops help students build the habits that matter most at university: reading mathematical texts with real understanding, explaining their ideas clearly and meeting unfamiliar problems with confidence. Rather than going back over the syllabus, we look at how students work with mathematics itself.

Workshops

Designed in conversation with the school.

Reading MathematicsLearning to read mathematical writing slowly and with understanding, rather than rushing to solve it.
Writing MathematicsLearning to put mathematical reasoning into clear, convincing words.
Preparing for University MathematicsHelping students cross from school mathematics to the demands of university study.

Workshops may be taken individually or as a connected series.
