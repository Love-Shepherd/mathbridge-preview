For schools

Mathematics beyond the classroom.

We work with schools that wish to extend their students’ mathematical education.

Our workshops build the habits that matter at university: reading mathematical texts with understanding, communicating ideas clearly and approaching unfamiliar problems with confidence. Rather than revisiting curriculum content, they focus on how students engage with mathematics itself.

Workshops

Designed in conversation with the school.

Reading MathematicsHelping students learn to read mathematical writing with understanding, and to approach it as something to interpret rather than only to solve.
Writing MathematicsDeveloping students’ ability to communicate mathematical reasoning clearly and construct convincing arguments.
Thinking Like a MathematicianEncouraging fuller mathematical discussion, richer problem solving and independent inquiry.
Preparing for University MathematicsSupporting students as they move from school mathematics to the demands of university study.

Workshops may be taken individually or as a connected series.
